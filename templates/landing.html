Our Secret Sauce

While the exact models and algorithms are a trade secret, our process is straightforward:

  1. The Opener: One AI provides the initial response.
  2. The Critiquer: A second AI, prompted to be skeptical, challenges the opener's logic and facts.
  3. The Synthesizer: A third, "judge" AI analyzes the debate and provides a final, synthesized "Golden Answer."

Our voting and weighting algorithms then refine this process over time. It’s not just a system; it’s a meritocracy of ideas.
